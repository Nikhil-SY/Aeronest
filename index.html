About Us

Aeronest Manufacturing & Engineering Pvt Ltd is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company established in 2025 by a team of experienced professionals. We cater to the ever-growing industrial sector where quality, precision, and timely delivery are critical.

We are a precision parts manufacturing company offering end-to-end solutions from raw material procurement and process documentation to post-machining processes, sub-assemblies, and fully finished mechanical kits.

Our Infrastructure

Our manufacturing facility is located in Peenya Industrial Area, Bengaluru, spread across 5000 sq. ft. and equipped with advanced machinery imported from the USA, Taiwan, and Japan.

A veteran team of engineers, skilled operators, and quality managers ensures robust process design, seamless execution, and uncompromising quality standards.

Quality Policy

At Aeronest, excellence is embedded in every detail. Our precision-engineered solutions undergo rigorous quality checks at every stage and are reliably delivered on schedule, consistently exceeding customer expectations.
